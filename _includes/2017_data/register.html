Eighteenth Meeting of the APEC Alliance for Supply Chain Connectivity (A2C2)

August 25, 2026 · Dalian, China

Convened on the margins of APEC 2026 and supported by the U.S. Department of State through the US APEC-RISE activity.

Join customs and trade officials, private sector representatives, and supply chain experts from across the Asia-Pacific region for the Eighteenth Meeting of the APEC Alliance for Supply Chain Connectivity (A2C2).

This year's meeting will examine the role of pre-arrival processing in improving supply chain efficiency, facilitating trade, and strengthening regional connectivity. By enabling customs authorities to review information before goods arrive, pre-arrival processing can reduce border delays, improve predictability for traders, and support more resilient supply chains.

The meeting will also feature updates from leaders of other APEC groups on cross-cutting initiatives and emerging priorities affecting regional supply chains.
